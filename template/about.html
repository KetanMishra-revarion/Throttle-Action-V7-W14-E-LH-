About Us Page

Throttle Action is a dynamic automotive media company headquartered in Bhubaneswar, Odisha, India. Our passion for cars drives us to provide engaging and informative content to automobile enthusiasts across the globe. With a keen eye for detail, we deliver comprehensive reviews, news, and features on the latest car models, trends, and technologies. From thrilling test drives to in-depth industry analysis, we strive to be the go-to destination for automotive enthusiasts seeking reliable and captivating content. Join us on our journey as we rev up the engine of automotive journalism, fueling the excitement and knowledge for all things on wheels.
